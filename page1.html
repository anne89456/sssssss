Apple Granola Yogurt with Honey Recipe

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • honey
  • cinnamon
  • alcohol
  • fire
  • oil/butter

Instructions

  1. Wash and Peel the banana
  2. Heat the oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the banana to the pan and cook for a few minutes until the bananas become crispy and cooked
  4. Flip the banana over and see it it's cooked
  5. Serve the fried bananas promptly, add cinnamon
  6. add a teaspoon of alcohol and burn the banana with the lighter
  7. close the lights so you will get a delicious and beautiful picture of banna on fire

Notes

  • You want bananas that are ripe but still firm. There should be no black spots or bruising on the peel, but no green spots either.
  • Honey varies tremendously in quality. Try to stay away from the cheap stuff that comes in a plastic bear bottle, and instead select a raw, unfiltered honey to use here. The flavor will be a million times better.

Enjoy your Fried banana with honey
