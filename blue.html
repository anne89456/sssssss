Blueberry Smoothies with Protein Powder Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Notes

  • You can use fresh blueberries instead of frozen, but the smoothie will be less thick and creamy.
  • You can use any type of protein powder you like, but vanilla is a classic choice.
  • You can use any type of milk you like, but unsweetened almond milk is a healthy and low-calorie option.
  • You can adjust the sweetness of the smoothie by adding more or less honey.
  • You can add other ingredients to this recipe, such as spinach, kale, or other fruits, to make it your own.

Enjoy your Blueberry Smoothies with Protein Powder!
