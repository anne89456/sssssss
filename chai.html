Taylor Swift's Chai Sugar Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar, plus more for topping
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 chai tea bag

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a cookie sheet.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the butter and vegetable oil together.
  3. Add the sugar, powdered sugar, egg, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Stir in the flour, baking soda, salt, and the contents of the chai tea bag.
  5. Roll the dough into balls and place them on the prepared cookie sheet.
  6. Sprinkle the tops of the cookies with sugar.
  7. Bake for 8-12 minutes, until the edges are lightly golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool on the cookie sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Notes

  • You can use eggnog instead of milk for the icing to make it more festive.
  • You can add food coloring to the icing to make it more colorful.
  • These cookies are perfect for fall and winter, and they make a great gift for friends and family.
