Iced Matcha Latte Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp matcha powder
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 cup milk - plant based or dairy
  • Ice
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make a matcha shot by whisking or shaking 2 tsp of matcha powder with 1/4 cup warm water until the mix is completely blended.
  2. Fill a tall glass with ice and pour in cold milk (plant based or dairy...our favorites are almond and oat).
  3. Top it off with your matcha shot.
  4. Add honey to sweeten, if desired.
  5. Stir and enjoy!

Notes

  • You can adjust the amount of matcha powder and honey to suit your taste.
  • You can use any type of milk you like, but almond and oat milk are popular choices.
  • You can add other ingredients to this recipe, such as vanilla extract or simple syrup, to make it your own.
