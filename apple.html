Apple Granola Yogurt with Honey Recipe

Ingredients

  • Granola
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 apples, cored and blended
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the granola, cinnamon, blended apples and honey until well combined.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the Greek yogurt and honey.
  3. Put the apple, yogurt, cinnamon and honey together in a serving bowl.
  4. Top with the granola and serve immediately.

Notes

  • You can use any type of apple you like, but tart apples work best for this recipe.
  • You can use any type of granola you like, but homemade granola is a healthy and delicious option.
  • You can adjust the sweetness of the granola and yogurt mixture by adding more or less honey.
  • You can add other ingredients to this recipe, such as nuts or dried fruit, to make it your own.

Enjoy your Apple Granola Yogurt with Honey!
