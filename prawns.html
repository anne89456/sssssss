Burmese Chilli Prawns with Hot & Sour Asian Slaw

Ingredients

  • 300g raw prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the prawns with turmeric powder, salt, and fish sauce.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add chilli flakes, tamarind paste, palm sugar, fish sauce, and lime juice. Cook until well combined.
  5. Add the marinated prawns and cook until they turn pink and are cooked through.
  6. Serve the Burmese Chilli Prawns with a garnish of coriander leaves.

Notes

  • You can adjust the level of spiciness by varying the amount of chilli flakes.
  • Feel free to customize this dish with your favorite vegetables or side dishes.

Enjoy your Burmese Chilli Prawns with Hot & Sour Asian Slaw!
